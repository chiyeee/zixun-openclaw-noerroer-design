What is Amazon FBA?

Definition

Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) is a service where Amazon stores, picks, packs, and ships products for sellers.

Step 1: Product Research in the AI Era

The days of scrolling through best-seller lists are over. In 2026, you need to leverage AI agents to analyze market sentiment.

Why Traditional Methods Fail

  • Saturation: Everyone uses the same filters on Helium 10.
  • False Signals: Giveaways distort BSR (Best Seller Rank).
  • Speed: Manual analysis takes weeks; trends last days.

The New "Agent-First" Approach

Instead of searching for keywords, you should be searching for problems.

  1. Identify a sub-niche with high complaint rates (use AI to scan reviews).
  2. Ask the Agent to summarize the top 3 recurring defects.
  3. Source a product that fixes exactly those 3 defects.

Case Study: Pet Supplies Category

We analyzed 5,000 reviews for "dog leashes" and found that "handle durability" was the #1 complaint.
