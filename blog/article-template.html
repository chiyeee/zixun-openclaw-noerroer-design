What is Amazon FBA?

Definition

Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) is a service where Amazon stores, picks, packs, and ships products for sellers, handling customer service and returns. Sellers send inventory to Amazon warehouses, and Amazon handles the logistics.

For most sellers, FBA is the default choice because it qualifies products for Amazon Prime shipping, which can increase conversion rates by up to 300%.
